03.01.02. Drivers ()
EN: Research competes for people and resources. Regulatory pressure and project-based working put pressure on focus and continuity. The driver is the sustainability of the system: less friction, better prioritisation, more basic security and shared facilities. NL: Onderzoek concurreert om mensen en middelen. Regeldruk en projectmatigheid zetten druk op focus en continuïteit. De driver is houdbaarheid van het systeem: minder frictie, betere prioritering, meer basiszekerheid en gedeelde voorzieningen.
Public legitimacy and broad prosperity (“licence to operate”) (Driver)
Resilience in a geopolitical and technological field of tension (Driver)
Reliability, reproducibility and acceleration of knowledge production (Driver)
Trust, values, and social acceptance (Driver)
Structural scarcity: money, time, talent and regulatory pressure (Driver)
Ecosystem strength and network effects (Driver)
Planetary limits and transition responsibility (Driver)
