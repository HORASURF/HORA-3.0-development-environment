Mission
We develop, validate and share reliable knowledge, applications and innovations, in collaboration with the scientific community, the education sector, professional practice, government, the business community and societal partners. In doing so, we contribute to better education, professional innovation, evidence-based policy and social impact in the areas of well-being, sustainability, safety and broad prosperity. (Mission)
