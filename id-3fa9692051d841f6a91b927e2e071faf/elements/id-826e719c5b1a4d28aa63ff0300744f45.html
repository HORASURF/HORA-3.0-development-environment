EN: Identifying, acquiring, allocating, administering, and accounting for research funds, including opportunity development, grant development, consortium formation, project design, program management, project control, progress monitoring, risk management, and reporting to funders and stakeholders. This also includes opportunity development, partner and consortium formation, project setup, progress management, risk management, and project accountability. This also includes project preparation, project setup, planning, work packages, governance, risk management, and progress management. This ensures that research is financially feasible, manageable, and accountable. For digital research facilities and research administrative systems, this requires explicit agreements on ownership, standards, integrations, data definitions, prioritization, and further development, so that a fragmented application and data landscape does not arise.
