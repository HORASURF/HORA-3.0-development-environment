Strategic research and continuity budget ()
EN: Budget for strategic flexibility and continuity within the research portfolio, such as start-up funding for new lines of research, proposal development, consortium formation, internal co-funding, matching funds, bridging funding and the temporary continuation of staff or facilities between external grants. This budget bridges the gap between long-term research ambitions and temporary or project-based forms of funding. Dit budget vangt de spanning op tussen structurele onderzoeksambities en tijdelijke of projectgebonden financieringsvormen.
